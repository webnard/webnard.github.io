Nikolay was a victim himself.

Since at least as early as 2007, and as recent as as at least 2012, Nikolay has cried out repeatedly for justice, but the government has done nothing but dismissed his cases.

If you are disaffected by the inability of the judicial system to bring to justice the middle-aged crooks destroying our country, then maybe it's time for vigilantism. There "may be" a huge bounty waiting as your reward.
